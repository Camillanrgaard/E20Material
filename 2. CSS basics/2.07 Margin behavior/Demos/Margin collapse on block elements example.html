Margin collapse example

The top and bottom margins of block elements are sometimes combined (collapsed) into a single margin whose size is the largest of the individual margins (or just one of them, if they are equal), a behavior known as margin collapsing.

Logically the margin between the boxes should be 20px but it is only 10px.

Box 1

     box-sizing: border-box; 
     border: 1px solid black;
     width: 125px;
     height: 100px;
     padding: 20px;
     margin: 10px;
